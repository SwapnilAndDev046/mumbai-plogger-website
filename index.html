How to Plog?

Plogging combines jogging with picking up litter. Here’s how you can get started:

  1. Gear Up: Wear comfortable running shoes and gloves to protect your hands.
  2. Choose a Route: Pick a path you enjoy, whether it's a park, beach, or neighborhood.
  3. Bring a Bag: Use a reusable bag to collect litter as you jog.
  4. Stay Safe: Be aware of your surroundings and avoid dangerous areas.
  5. Dispose of Waste Properly: Make sure to sort and dispose of the collected litter responsibly. Recycle where possible!
  6. Enjoy the Experience: Plogging is a great way to exercise while making your community cleaner!
