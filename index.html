About Us

Vision

Mumbai Ploggers, as an initiative, aims to mobilize individuals in the cities of Mumbai and Navi Mumbai and sensitize our communities towards the issues of waste management and environmental conservation.

Rashmi Singh

Founder, Mumbai Ploggers

Mumbai Ploggers, founded by Rashmi Singh in November 2020 at age 23, aims to raise awareness about waste management at natural attractions in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai. As a passionate advocate of the UN's sustainable development goals, Rashmi believes in creating lasting change through education and mindset shifts for a better, sustainable future.
